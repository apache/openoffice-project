Press Inquiries

Press Inquiries¶

The Apache OpenOffice Project Management Committee welcomes inquiries from the press. We can be contacted via email at press@openoffice.apache.org.

For members of the media and analyst communities with questions of a broader Apache nature, please contact the ASF Press Team directly.

Unofficial Communication¶

Unless otherwise specified, the Apache OpenOffice community members, including committers and Project Management Committee members, always express their personal opinions when they post to mailing lists or forums, write articles on personal blogs, post comments on news websites or blogs or use social networks.

Personal opinions do not necessarily reflect the views of the Apache OpenOffice project. For official responses the press should inquire by e-mailing press@openoffice.apache.org.
